Privacy Policy - SVZ: Special Forces Against Zombies

This privacy policy applies to the game "SVZ: Special Forces Against Zombies" (hereinafter referred to as "the Game") developed by [SVZ Team].

1. Information Collection

The Game does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. All game data is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers. The Game does not use any tracking technologies and does not request access to your advertising identifier (IDFA).

2. Third-Party Services

The Game may integrate the following third-party services:

  • Apple’s Game Center: Optional for leaderboards and achievements. Data is handled by Apple under their privacy policy.
  • Ad Networks [e.g., Google AdMob]: Used to display non-personalized ads. May collect limited non-identifiable data such as device type and IP address for ad delivery. We do not enable personalized advertising or user tracking. Please refer to Google's privacy policy.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties.

3. Children’s Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
